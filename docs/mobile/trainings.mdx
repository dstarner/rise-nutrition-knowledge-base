# Trainings

---

Trainings is a place to log all matches, games, exercises, practices, and other physical activities an athlete participates in.

## Add a Training for Yourself

Individuals often train on their own time or are responsible for tracking their own workouts.

The following steps can be taken to track a training session:

<ImageStepper>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Open Trainings"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/homescreen.jpg"
    imgCaption="From the main screen, tap Training"
  />
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Track a Training"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/track_training_btn.jpg"
    imgCaption="Tap “+ Track Training” in the bottom right corner"
  />
</ImageStepper>

### Training Category

Tap the arrow to the right of the section labelled _Training Category_ and select the category from the drop-down menu that best describes your training.

<ImageStepper>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Training Categories"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/training_category_dd.jpg"
    imgCaption="Open the Training Categories selections"
  />
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Select Choice"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/training_type_choices.png"
    imgCaption="Select a training category"
  />
</ImageStepper>

### Training Type

Tap the arrow to the right of the section labelled _Training Type_ and select the type from the drop-down
menu that best describes your training or select **Other**.

<ImageStepper>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Training Types"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/training_type_dd.jpg"
    imgCaption="Open the Training Types selections"
  />
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Select Choice"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/training_category_choices.png"
    imgCaption="Select a training type"
  />
</ImageStepper>

### Session Name

Provide a name for the training session so that it can be found easily later.

Some examples could be:

* _10-Mile Run_
* _Walk on Tuesday, May 12th_
* _Game against the Crusaders_
* _Lift with Coach_

### Date & Time

<ImageStepper>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Open Date Picker"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/training_start_date.png"
    imgCaption="Open the datepicker"
  >

If the date of your training is different than the date displayed to the right of _Start Date_, tap
the displayed date.

  </ImageStepper.Step>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Select Date"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/training_start_date_open.png"
    imgCaption="Choose the new date"
  >

Change the month shown by tapping the arrows in the top right to toggle between
different months. Tap the drop down arrow to the right of the month and year displayed in the top-left
corner to change the year. Click on the date you'd like to choose once it is displayed on your screen.

  </ImageStepper.Step>
</ImageStepper>

<ImageStepper>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Open Time Picker"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/training_start_time.jpg"
    imgCaption="Open the timepicker"
  >

If the time of your training is different than the time displayed to the right of _Start Time_, tap the time displayed.

  </ImageStepper.Step>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Select Choice"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/training_start_time_open.png"
    imgCaption="Choose the new time"
  >

Change the hour and minutes shown by tapping and dragging the hour and minutes to scroll to the correct time.

  </ImageStepper.Step>
</ImageStepper>

### Change Duration

The session duration is how long you were active during the training.

<ImageStepper>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Select Duration Inputs"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/duration.jpg"
  >

If the duration of your training is different than what is displayed, tap on the fields under hours
and minutes and type the correct duration in the field box.

  </ImageStepper.Step>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Edit Durations"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/duration_change.png"
  >

Change the hour and minutes shown by typing to the correct time on your keyboard.

  </ImageStepper.Step>
</ImageStepper>

### Saving

Tap the check mark in the top right corner to save your training.


![Save training](/img/docs/mobile/trainings/training_save.jpg)

After saving the training, you can and **should** provide feedback so you and your coaches can see
your activity levels and responses over time.


## Provide Session Feedback

When participating in a training session, try to provide feedback after finishing your activities. This helps
your trainers, coaches, dietitians, medical staff, and others to better understand how your training went.

### Selecting a Training

<ImageStepper>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Open Trainings"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/homescreen.jpg"
    imgCaption="From the main screen, tap Training"
  />
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Select a Training"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/select-training-feedback.png"
    imgCaption="Tap the training for which you'd like to provide feedback"
  >

With the trainings displayed, select a training by clicking it's list item. A feedback-requested
icon is displayed on the right of a training if you still need to provide feedback.

  </ImageStepper.Step>
</ImageStepper>

### Recovery Rating Questions

Answer the recovery rating questions regarding training zone, energy, fatigue, cramping, and soreness. 

<ImageStepper>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Answer Questions"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/feedback_dropdown.jpg"
    imgCaption="Select a response for each dropdown"
  >

Tap the drop-down arrow to the right of each section and select the best answer for that category.

  </ImageStepper.Step>
  <ImageStepper.Step
    title="Save Responses"
    imgSrc="/img/docs/mobile/trainings/save_feedback_response.jpg"
    imgCaption="Save your responses"
  >

Tap **Save Responses** on the bottom of the screen once you have answered all questions appropriately.

  </ImageStepper.Step>
</ImageStepper>

Your feedback is now saved and viewable by your coaches, trainers, dietitians, and other dashboard users associated with your team.
