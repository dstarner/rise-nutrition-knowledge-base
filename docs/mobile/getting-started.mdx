# Downloading the Athlete Mobile App

---

The mobile application is currently available on iOS and Android devices. 

App Name: **Rise Nutrition Athletes**

## Downloading for iOS

Download the mobile app from [the Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/rise-nutrition-athletes/id1586753485).
You must have an account and be associated with a team to enter the app's features.


## Downloading for Android

Download the mobile app from [the Google Play Store(https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.athleteapp).
You must have an account and be associated with a team to enter the app's features.
